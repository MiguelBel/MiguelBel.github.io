Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 19, 2025 | Last Updated: November 19, 2025

Introduction

NoHangxiety ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our hangover anxiety relief application.

Information We Collect

Personal Wellness Data

  • Mood Tracking: We may collect information about your mood and wellness entries when you use our tracking features
  • Usage Patterns: We collect data about how you interact with the app to provide personalized recommendations
  • Health Preferences: Settings and preferences you configure within the app

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data (anonymized)

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email address, or phone number unless you provide it for support
  • We do not track your precise location
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, or other personal data
  • We do not share your personal wellness data with third-party advertisers

How We Use Your Information

Local Processing

  • Most data processing occurs locally on your device
  • Your mood and wellness entries are stored securely on your device
  • Personalized recommendations are generated using on-device algorithms

App Improvement

  • Anonymized usage data helps us improve app features and user experience
  • Crash reports help us identify and fix technical issues
  • Performance metrics help us optimize the app

Optional Cloud Sync

  • If you enable cloud sync, your wellness data may be stored in secure cloud storage
  • Cloud data is encrypted and accessible only to you
  • You can disable cloud sync at any time

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal wellness data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Limited Sharing

  • Analytics: We may use privacy-focused analytics services to understand app usage (anonymized data only)
  • Service Providers: Trusted service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

Protection Measures

  • All wellness data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • We use industry-standard security protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No direct access to your personal wellness data by our team

Your Device Security

  • Data on your device is protected by iOS security features
  • We recommend using device passcodes and biometric authentication
  • Enable automatic updates to receive security patches

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: View all your wellness data within the app
  • Export: Export your data in a portable format
  • Delete: Delete specific entries or all your data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and crash reporting in settings

Account Deletion

  • You can delete all your data by clearing app data in settings
  • Deleting the app removes all local data
  • Cloud data (if enabled) can be deleted from your account settings
  • Contact us at miguelbeltransanz@gmail.com to request complete data deletion

Third-Party Services

NoHangxiety may integrate with third-party services:

  • Apple HealthKit: Optional integration to sync wellness data (with your explicit permission)
  • Analytics Services: Privacy-focused analytics (anonymized data only)
  • Each third-party service has its own privacy policy

Children's Privacy

NoHangxiety is intended for users 21 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 21. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email (if provided). Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

European Privacy Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
