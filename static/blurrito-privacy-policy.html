Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2025 | Last Updated: July 4, 2025

Introduction

Blurrito ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our photo blur editing application.

Information We Collect

Photos and Camera Access

  • Photos: We access your photo library only when you select photos to edit
  • Camera: We access your camera only when you choose to take new photos within the app
  • Purpose: Photos are accessed solely for editing and applying blur effects

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not collect usage analytics or crash reports
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, or other personal data

How We Use Your Information

Local Processing Only

  • All photo editing is performed locally on your device
  • Photos are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party services
  • Blur effects are applied using your device's processing power
  • No data leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share your edited photos

Photo Storage

  • Original photos remain unchanged in your photo library
  • Edited photos are saved to your device's photo library only when you choose to save them
  • You have full control over saving and sharing your edited photos

Data Sharing

We do not share your photos or any personal information with third parties.

  • No photos are transmitted to external servers
  • No analytics are collected or shared
  • No advertising networks have access to your data
  • No third-party services process your photos

Data Security

Device-Level Security

  • All processing occurs on your device using Apple's secure frameworks
  • Photos are handled using Apple's PhotosUI framework with standard security protocols
  • No network connections are made for photo processing
  • Your edited photos are protected by your device's built-in security features

Your Rights and Choices

Photo Access Control

  • You can revoke photo library access at any time through iOS Settings
  • You can revoke camera access at any time through iOS Settings
  • The app will request permission before accessing photos or camera

Data Deletion

  • Delete the app to remove all associated data
  • Original photos remain in your library (unchanged)
  • Saved edited photos can be deleted from your photo library like any other photo

Children's Privacy

Blurrito is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
